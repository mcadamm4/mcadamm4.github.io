For this project we had a group of five, the objective here was to develop an app idea and instead of actually making the app we had to market it through the construction of a website and by making a poster. The app we decided on was a sort of communication hub between students of the Clubs/Socs of their college where they get alerts about events and can sign up to Clubs/Socs in the app instead of being bombarded with emails. I done a lot of the core work for this project in consultation with the others in the group. I designed and built the site and the others worked on the content of the pages.
