The goal of this project was to work in groups of three/four to design and build a website of our own choice. There were certain restrictions to adhere to like the website cannot be a fan page to your favourite band/team. There cannot be any inflammatory or pornagrpahic content and it cannot be an evetn page for nightclubs in Dublin etc. We decided that to design a well rounded and content rich website a good focus would be reviews. We each chose a genre of media that we were interested in and designed a featurefull site with the use of HTML, CSS and Javascript.
